Luxe is an award winning home design and project management firm.

Co-founded by John Nicklas and Brent Hugus, Luxe is based firmly upon a foundation and commitment to innovative products and unmatched design integrity. We provide a complete project platform that includes design, product resource, installation and project management.

Luxe has two distinct locations. The Market Square showroom at 200 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, is a beautiful downtown site that has been in service since 2010. Joining Market Square, is the new Indigo Square showroom in East Liberty. This 3,000 sq ft space includes the latest technology in the kitchen, plumbing, tile and lighting industries. Highlighted in the vast array of kitchen and bathroom settings are famed international and domestic manufacturers.

Our showrooms create an environment where our clients can come to be completely inspired by the possibilities for creating a remarkable kitchen and bath; two vital areas in the home that can be equally functional as well as beautifully designed. We want to share our passion for design and exceptional product with our customers.
